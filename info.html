The Strike Games Website is a website, made by Google Sites in September 2022. This site was made by the owner of the Strike Games YouTube channel, made in 2016.
After the site's creation, it was only known to a few people. One day, a tournament was planned, and when it was sent to more than 50 people via gmail, the site boomed in popularity.
There are also equally popular sites, like Math180 (not the learning site, the gaming site also made via Google Sites) and BAA BAA SHEEP, which is was also made with Google Sites.
There are also Unions, which are smaller sites.
